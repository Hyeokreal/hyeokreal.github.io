Introduction

This is a demo website for paper “Music source separation for generative adversarial networks”

Abstract

Music source separation is an important task because of its potential of being used in many applications in music information retrieval field. In this paper, we propose a novel approach using generative adversarial networks(GANs) to solve this task. We apply Wasserstein GANs in conditional GANs fashion to extract the vocal and accompaniment conditioning on the mixture. As a deep learning model, we use U-net architecture whose layers are composed of convolutional neural networks. The experiment shows that it achieves meaningful results on the SiSEC DSD100 dataset.
